Weekly Study Guide

Recruit Study Assignments (verbatim definitions, crime elements, case law, radio codes, phonetic alphabet) must be completed before the start of the week listed — the week number is the material you're responsible for during that week's inspections and tests.

Verbatim definitions are memorized word-for-word and begin/end with "Sir" or "Ma'am." Elements of definitions and crime elements don't have to be verbatim, but every element must be stated, also beginning with "Sir" or "Ma'am."
